    Bolo boards are written in a little language called BoardCode. It can be a bit daunting at first, but once you get the hang of it, it sure makes board-writing easier.

    You may want to read the docs; BoardCode : A Primer.

    Block Types

    Metric – A single number, with [optional] coloring thresholds.

    Sparkline – A small plot with a label, no units and no axes.

    Graph – One or more lines or [stacked] areas, color-coded. This is what most people thing of when they hear "monitoring".
